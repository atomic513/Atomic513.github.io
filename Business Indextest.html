Our Values

 Ever since I was a child I wanted to make a lot of money. I wanted 30 dabloons, 43 yugnars, -12 bumbershoot and at least 20 brengelleng. I figured the best way to do this was to start a business. The Thing Place is very special to both me and my employees because we are proud of the fact that we don't have to pay taxes since we are legally considered to be a church. Our customers are so satisfied with us that we don't even need a complaints department! Please stop calling us to find the number for it. It doesn't exist.
 People always ask me, how are your services so cheap? You must barely be keeping afloat. How do you even manage to pay your workers?
 The simplest answer is that we don't! Since we are a church, our workers are all volunteers! You can become one as well!
